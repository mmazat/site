3.5 Inch Drone

January 01, 2025



I constructed a 3.5-inch drone following this YouTube Video. Using the INav configurator, I flashed the firmware and installed an optical flow and range sensor (Matek 3901) with guidance from this video. These sensors help stabilize the drone's position and altitude. I disabled the GPS and magnetometer in INav and performed extensive PID tuning to achieve stable position mode.
