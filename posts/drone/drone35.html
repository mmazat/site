3.5 Inch Drone

January 01, 2025



I built a 3.5-inch drone following this video. Using the INav configurator, I flashed the firmware and installed an optical flow and range sensor (Matek 3901) with guidance from this video. These sensors help stabilize the drone's position and altitude. I disabled the GPS and magnetometer in INav and did a lot of PID tuning to achieve stable position mode. Ability to hold altitude and position is very helpful for a beginner pilot.
